Features

Everything you need to see your portfolio clearly

Portavio auto-detects risks, maps ownership, and tracks velocity — all without changing how your teams use Jira.

⚠️

Auto-Detect Risks & Blockers

No manual tagging required. Portavio automatically identifies risks and blockers from Jira labels, issue status, and priorities.

👥

Team → Project → Epic Mapping

Explicit ownership relationships. See exactly which teams own which epics, and how work is distributed across projects.

📈

Velocity & Predictability

Track team velocity over time and calculate predictability scores. Identify which teams deliver consistently vs. which are struggling.

🔗

Cross-Project Dependencies

Visual dependency graph shows how epics block each other across teams and projects. Spot bottlenecks before they cause delays.

🎯

Real-Time Portfolio Health

Live dashboard shows project health, epic progress, team load, and open risks — all in one place, always up to date.

🚀

5-Minute Setup

Connect your Jira instance, sync once, and you're done. No plugins to install, no workflow changes, no training required.
